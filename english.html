ENG4U: English

I have a love hate relationship with english. It could be my best subject, or my worst. Throughout elementary school, I never could finish my work and teachers would have to pull me aside. My sister would tell me that I would have parent-teacher interviews all the time and they said I was slow to write. I don't think I was incompetent, I just never wanted to write down my ideas. Fast forward to Grade 8, I joined my school's newspaper club where I revised and designed issues. It was the first time I wrote for fun and I loved writing the introductions to each issue. Today, I do a lot of communication, but I do want to get into journalism at some point!
