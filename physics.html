SPH4U: Physics

I love physics, but that doesn't mean I'm good at it. When I took phyiscs in grade 11, I either did really good or really bad on a test. It was hard learning in that class because my teacher loves physics. You think that made the class easier, but he would not stop talking. He overteaches and goes on tangents and we all tune him out. I spend time in class filling out the note, and then learning his lessons online because he's more straightforward. No diss to him though, he does round up your grade a lot. I'm excited for physics next year though since a lot of the graduates mentioned it was their favourite memory in high school. Maybe it will be mine too.
